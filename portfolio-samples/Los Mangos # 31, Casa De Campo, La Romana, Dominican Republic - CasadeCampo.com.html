Description

Nestled at the end of a cul-de-sac just steps from Minitas Beach, lies the historic “Tropicalia” estate. Completely private and surrounded by majestic vegetation, the estate boasts a private driveway, an inviting lap pool and an enormous garden complete with ponds and numerous gazebos. Additionally, the 7-bedroom residence boasts a fully air-conditioned living room, an over-sized media room, two kitchens, a gym, and a safe-room. One-of-a-kind and priced to sell, this estate has hosted countless dignitaries and celebrities throughout the years.
